What Is Play For 60

Physical activity is one of the most important parts of a child's health, wellbeing and learning. Helping them instill it into their lives is a huge priority in modern parenting.

Play for 60 is a program that helps nurture interest in sports among children at a young age and provides the recommended 60 minutes of exercise through programs like sports sampling and team challenges.

Online

Children can now stay active while indoors, with our 60 minute virtual sessions held by various Dubai Sports Academies.

On Ground

As the city of Dubai continues to fight COVID-19 and opens up at groundbreaking speeds, our on ground version ensures children are playing and engaged after school in a safe, fun and healthy environment, getting their daily 60 minutes of physical activity in their communities.
